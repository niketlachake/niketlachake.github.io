Research Understanding

Bonded Bush

BOND FAILURE IN BONDED BUSHES

Rubber Parts

Bonded bush makes a big contribution in the performance of the vehicle. It consists of two tubes with rubber bonded between them. They are designed to be used as vibration isolators for vehicles. They go through several load and have to be tested accordingly one of which is Bonding Test. In this the inner tube go through application of load on it which shears out the rubber and makes the breakage of the link between the rubber and the tube. In this test if proper metal preparation is not done it leads to Rubber to Metal Failure where the rubber is not bonded to the tubes properly.

Anti Vibration Mounts

RELATIONSHIP BETWEEN THE STIFFNESS AND DAMPING

Design & Development

The antivibration mounts have been part of vehicle from long time. The main task of it is to reduce the vibration from the engine to the body/ chassis. In this damping of the vibration plays a crucial role in the NVH operation. The damping of the mount is related to the stiffness of the mount as the ability of the mount to ansorb the shock is more in the higher stiffness mounts.

Rubber Testing

Elasticity

Testing Development

Rubber is the material with high elasticity, low thermal and electric conductivity. The hardness of the rubber plays a vital role with the elongation factor. The elastic limit of the rubber gets hampered with higher hardness and also the performance of the rubber parts in the vehicles. Each hardness has its own stiffness curves based on the which we can select a proper rubber for the operation purpose.
